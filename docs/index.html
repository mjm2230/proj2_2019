All websites must have these 4 design elements.

Most important stuff is the biggest stuff.

Minimal distractions with respect to color, font, and media.

White space is your friend.

For this site, I chose a simple color palette of three shades of teal and white. I use two simple fonts, one for titles and one for regular text. All items in the grid itself have their own row except the three principles I used to design this site; since those three principles are of equal importance, they are on the same line. In a mobile browser, however, each principle is in its own row for easiser reading. I chose to put an image at the top of the site so that you have to scroll down to see more detailed content after arriving at the site. Finally, I chose to include a nav bar and a footer for ease of navigation and consistency if I were to further build up the site.
